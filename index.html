What is neurodiversity?

Neurodiversity is the concept that neurological differences, such as ADHD, autism, dyslexia, giftedness and many others, are natural variations of the human brain rather than disorders to be cured.

It emphasizes the value of diverse ways of thinking and challenges stigmatization by advocating for acceptance and inclusion.

Non-linear thinking — a gift, a curse, or both?

At Neurodisruptors, we believe that no one has a deficit or a disorder. We operate with a Spiky Profile.

We use the term "neurodivergence" simply because it's widely recognized, but from our perspective, it's a gift rather than a limitation. A non-linear brain has given us heightened creativity, resilience, out-of-the-box thinking, passion and a unique way of viewing the world.

Instead of seeing neurodivergence as a challenge, we embrace non-linear thinking more as a superpower that fuels innovation, drives our entrepreneurial spirit, and helps us break boundaries.

Yes, some of us may see non-linear thinking as a curse, some others as a gift, some struggle a lot, some less, and some not at all. But is this not the essence of being human?
