PhD Opportunity at IESE

Exploring the Power of Neurodiversity for Entrepreneurs and High Effective Leaders 🧠

IESE Business School is seeking a brilliant PhD candidate passionate about neurodiversity research. If you're a neurodivergent individual (or linked to neurodiversity in any way) with a strong academic background and a desire to explore the intersection of neurodiversity and business, we want to hear from you!

This exciting opportunity will allow you to delve into cutting-edge research on neurodivergence, potentially focusing on topics like ADHD, dyslexia, and others, within the dynamic context of a leading business school. You'll contribute to a growing body of knowledge and help shape a more inclusive future for neurodivergent individuals in the business world.

If you thrive in a challenging intellectual environment, are driven by curiosity, and possess excellent analytical and communication skills, this could be the perfect fit. Please share this post with anyone who could be interested.

Do you want more information? Please DM us for details about the position and application process. Let's work together to create a more neuroinclusive world!
