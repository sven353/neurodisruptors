Our Team

A diverse group of non-linear professionals

United by a common purpose: unleashing our neurodivergent genius to transform the future of innovation and inclusion.

Our team includes individuals sharing neurodivergence symptoms, some with partners, colleagues, or friends living with a non-linear brain. Some are also researchers passionate about exploring the unique strengths that neurodivergence brings. Together, we believe that non-linear thinking, far from being a limitation, can be a powerful engine for innovation and creativity.

As a community, we welcome anyone who shares our purpose and wants to contribute to pushing boundaries in this space. Whether you have direct experience with neurodivergence, share a non-linear brain, or simply feel inspired to support our mission, there's a place for you here. Let's collectively shape a future where neurodivergent strengths are at the forefront of progress.

If you're passionate about this purpose, we invite you to join us!
