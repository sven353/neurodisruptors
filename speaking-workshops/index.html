Speaking & Workshops

Non-linear leadership, for HR and leadership teams

Most companies still design roles, evaluation systems, and leadership expectations around steady cognitive output and predictable execution. When non-linear minds — ADHD, giftedness, dyslexia — operate inside those systems, friction appears. Not because those individuals lack capacity, but because the structures around them were never designed with their operating systems in mind.

Dr. Sven Mulfinger works with HR teams, leadership teams, and boards to make that architecture visible, and to show what changes once it is.

Formats

Keynote

ADHD, Giftedness, and Dyslexia in the C-Suite. Why the traits that get read as inconsistency or lack of discipline in top performers are often a distinct, describable cognitive architecture — and what changes once leaders see it.

Workshop

Why Boards Misread Non-Linear Leaders as Unreliable. A working session for boards and senior leadership on reading variability correctly — and why burnout in high performers usually reflects architecture, not personality.

Team session

Designing Teams Around Cognitive Spikes Instead of Managing Them Away. Practical session for people managers and HR/L&D on structuring roles, feedback, and expectations around how non-linear minds actually perform.

Formats above can be adapted in length and depth — from a 45-minute keynote to a half-day workshop. Exact pricing and availability shared on request.
