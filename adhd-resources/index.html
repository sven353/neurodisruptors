ADHD Resources and more

Practical resources for ADHD focus, productivity, and thriving

Curated books, podcasts, clinical resources, and research on ADHD and entrepreneurship — from work hacks to lifestyle tips, we've got you covered.

The Non-Linear Operating System

This book makes visible how non-linear minds actually process, decide, and create value under real conditions. It shows why traditional systems fail many high-capacity individuals, and how patterns like ADHD, giftedness, and dyslexia shape both strengths and friction.

From that recognition, it gives you a way to design your work, decisions, and environment around energy, not time.

Organizations & expert resources

CHADD

Children and Adults with ADHD. One of the largest and most comprehensive resources for ADHD: educational materials, research updates, and a support group directory, plus resources for adults including webinars and tips for relationships and work.

ADDA

Attention Deficit Disorder Association. Focuses on adult ADHD with evidence-based information on time management, productivity, career advice, relationships, and emotional regulation, plus webinars, virtual support groups, and podcasts.

ADDitude

The ADHD Experts Magazine & Podcast. A trusted source sharing expert advice from psychologists, psychiatrists, and ADHD coaches, featuring interviews with leading ADHD professionals on managing ADHD as an adult.

Clinical resources

  • US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention — free materials and resources on ADHD for families, teachers, healthcare providers, and public health professionals.
  • ADHD symptom checker: Clinical Partners — understand ADHD better, including symptoms, diagnostic processes, and effective treatment options.
